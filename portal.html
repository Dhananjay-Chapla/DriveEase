Step-by-Step Guide

📹 Visual Guide

Watch this quick tutorial to understand the entire process of applying for your Learner Licence before you begin.

1
Fill Application Form (Form 1 / 1A)
Provide personal details, address, and vehicle class. Ensure Aadhaar-linked mobile is active.
📎 Aadhaar Card📎 Age Proof📎 Address Proof
2
Upload Photo & Signature
Upload a recent passport-size photo (white background) and scanned signature in JPG format.
3
Pay Application Fee Online
₹200 for 2-wheeler, ₹300 for LMV. Pay via UPI, Net Banking, Debit/Credit Card.
4
Book LL Test Slot
Choose a convenient RTO and time slot for your Learner Licence computerised test.
5
Appear for LL Test & Get Certificate
Pass the online MCQ test at the RTO. LL is valid for 6 months — use it to practice driving.

📹 Visual Guide

Watch this step-by-step tutorial on how to smoothly apply for your permanent Driving Licence.

1
Prerequisite: Valid Learner Licence (30+ days old)
You must have held your LL for at least 30 days before applying for a Driving Licence.
2
Fill DL Application Form 9
Enter LL number, personal details, and select vehicle class (e.g. LMV, MCWG, HMV).
📎 LL Certificate📎 Medical Certificate📎 Form 1A
3
Book Driving Test Slot
Select your nearest RTO and preferred time slot for the driving skill test.
4
Pay Fee & Appear for Test
Fee: ₹200–₹500 depending on vehicle class. Inspector evaluates your driving skills.
5
DL Dispatched to Registered Address
Smart card DL is printed and dispatched via Speed Post within 7–10 working days.

📹 Visual Guide

Need help with DL Renewal? Watch our contactless eKYC renewal walkthrough.

1
Check DL Expiry Date
Apply for renewal within 1 year before expiry or up to 5 years after. Late fees may apply.
2
eKYC Verification (Contactless)
If your Aadhaar details match DL records, renewal is fully contactless — no RTO visit needed!
📎 Aadhaar📎 Medical Certificate (40+ years)
3
Pay Renewal Fee Online
₹200 per vehicle class. Pay via UPI/Net Banking. Confirm payment receipt.
4
Renewed DL Delivered to Your Door
Smart card dispatched in 7–10 days. Track via application status page.

📹 Visual Guide

Planning to drive abroad? Watch our guide on securing an International Driving Permit safely and easily.

1
Eligibility Check
Must hold a valid Indian Driving Licence. IDP is valid for 1 year or until DL expiry.
📎 Valid DL📎 Passport📎 Visa
2
Fill Form 4A Online
Submit details of country of travel and travel dates. Upload passport and travel documents.
3
Visit RTO Once for Collection
IDP is issued on the same day at the RTO after verification. Bring all originals.
